About Surya City – Surya City: Builders
& Developers in Bharatpur

An eco-friendly group housing complex named Surya City is being constructed on a plot of land measuring 106 Bigha situated nearby Agra Road NH-11, Bharatpur. Surya City will offer affordable luxury homes in various combinations besides Plots, Flats, Villa, 2 BHK, 3BHK luxury Apartments. A private club, well-designed Parks, Gymnasium, Tennis court, Spa, etc. will provide additional indulgence to the residents.

The project was launched in the April 2010 year and has received an overwhelming response from the buyers. The construction work on the site is in full swing and the project will be completed as per plans.

We at Surya are committed to exploring new Residential & Commercial Properties for Sale in Bharatpur dimensions to accomplish clients, new methods of doing business and the utilization of new technologies.
